Dear Driver and Operator Team,

I am writing to enquire about the approval process for an electronic logbook application under the Land Transport Rule: Work Time and Logbooks 2007.

We have developed **RoadLog**, a web-based electronic logbook designed for New Zealand commercial drivers. The application is currently in a working prototype stage and we would appreciate your guidance on the formal approval requirements.

**About RoadLog:**

RoadLog is a mobile-friendly web application that enables commercial drivers to digitally record their work time, driving hours, and rest breaks in compliance with NZTA regulations. Key features include:

- Real-time tracking of driving time, work time, and break periods
- Configurable driver-type selection (Goods Service — 5.5hr limit / Passenger Service — 7hr limit)
- Automated compliance warnings before limits are reached
- GPS location capture at each shift event with reverse geocoding
- Odometer/distance recorder input at shift start and end
- SHA-256 hash chain tamper-proofing to ensure data integrity
- Fortnightly driving limit tracking (70 hours / 14 days)
- PDF and CSV export for roadside inspections and audit purposes
- Enforcement View designed for officer access during inspections
