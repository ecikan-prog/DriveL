## Getting Started

### 1. Download & Register

1. Download **Drive Legal** from the App Store (iPhone) or Google Play (Android)
2. Tap **"Create Account"**
3. Enter your details:
   - Full name (as on your driver licence)
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 6 characters)
   - NZ Driver Licence number
   - Vehicle registration number
   - Vehicle type (Rideshare/Taxi, Van, Truck, Bus, Heavy Vehicle, Other)
   - Driver type: **Goods Service** (5.5hr break rule) or **Passenger Service** (7hr break rule)
4. Tap **"Create Account"** — you're ready to go!

> **Free Trial:** You get 14 days free. After that, subscribe for NZD $4.99/month or NZD $39.99/year.

---

### 2. Start Your Shift

When you begin work for the day:

1. Open Drive Legal
2. Tap the **"New Entry"** tab (the + button in the centre of the tab bar)
3. Tap **"Start Shift"** — enter your starting odometer reading if prompted
4. The circular countdown timer on the Dashboard starts counting down your remaining driving time
5. The screen stays awake while your shift is active

**What you'll see on the Dashboard:**

The Dashboard shows a large circular green ring — your **hours remaining until rest break** is required. Below the timer you'll see when your shift started, and a stats row showing today's hours, this week's hours, and your fortnightly total.

---

### 3. Take a Break

When you stop for a break (meal, rest, fuel):

1. Tap the **"New Entry"** tab
2. Tap **"Start Break"** — your break timer starts
3. When you're ready to drive again, tap **"End Break"**
4. Your countdown timer resumes

**NZTA Rules to Remember:**

| Driver Type | Break Rule | Daily Maximum | Fortnightly Maximum |
|---|---|---|---|
| Passenger Service | 30-min break after **7 hours** driving | 13 hours work | 70 hours |
| Goods Service | 30-min break after **5.5 hours** driving | 13 hours work | 70 hours |

> Drive Legal will warn you with alerts before you hit these limits!

---

### 4. End Your Shift

When you finish work for the day:

1. Tap the red **"END SHIFT"** button on the Dashboard (or via New Entry tab)
2. Enter your ending odometer reading when prompted
3. Your shift summary appears showing total driving, work, and break time
4. The shift is automatically saved to your history and synced to the cloud
5. A tamper-proof SHA-256 hash is generated — the record cannot be edited later

---

### 5. View Your Logbook

Tap the **Logbook** tab to see:

- All your past shifts listed by date
- Total driving hours, work hours, and breaks for each day
- Filter by: This Week, This Fortnight, This Month, or All Time
- Colour-coded status: 🟢 Compliant, 🟡 Near limit, 🔴 Over limit

---

### 6. Export Your Records

Tap the **Reports** tab to access exports:

- Tap **📄 Export PDF** — generates a professional logbook report with GPS locations, odometer readings, and your driver details
- Tap **📥 Export CSV** — exports raw data for your employer or payroll

The PDF includes your name, licence number, vehicle rego, GPS start/end locations, odometer readings, and a full shift table — ready to hand to your employer or keep for your records.

---

### 7. Show to a Transport Officer

During a roadside inspection:

1. Tap the **Reports** tab
2. Tap **🔒 Enforcement View**
3. Hand your phone to the officer
4. They'll see:
   - Your driver details and licence number
   - Verification status (✅ = records are tamper-proof)
   - Last 14 days of shift records with GPS locations
   - Fortnightly driving summary
5. If they need a copy, tap **"Export PDF"** to generate one instantly

> **Tip:** The green ✅ VERIFIED badge means your records haven't been tampered with. Officers can trust these records.

## Warning Alerts

| Alert | What It Means | What to Do |
|---|---|---|
| 🟡 "Approaching driving limit" | You've been driving 6+ hours (Passenger) or 5+ hours (Goods) | Plan your next break |
| 🔴 "Take a 30-minute break NOW!" | You've hit your driving limit | Stop immediately and rest for 30+ minutes |
| 🔴 "10-hour rest break required!" | You've worked 13 hours | End your shift and rest for 10+ hours |
| 🟡 "Approaching 70-hour fortnightly limit" | You're near 70 hours for the fortnight | Plan your 24-hour rest period |

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What if my phone dies during a shift?**
Your shift data is saved locally every time you take an action (start break, end break). When your phone restarts, open Drive Legal — your active shift will still be running.

**Q: Can I edit a past shift?**
No. Once a shift is ended, it's locked with a tamper-proof SHA-256 hash. This is required by NZTA to ensure records are trustworthy.

**Q: What happens after my 14-day trial?**
You'll be prompted to subscribe (NZD $4.99/month or NZD $39.99/year). You can still view your past records, but you won't be able to start new shifts until you subscribe.

**Q: Do I need internet to use the app?**
No. Drive Legal works completely offline. You only need internet to download the app, process subscription payments, and sync your records to the cloud backup.

**Q: Is my data safe?**
Yes. All data is stored locally on your device and automatically backed up to an encrypted cloud database. Records are secured with SHA-256 cryptographic hashes that detect any tampering.

**Q: I have a new phone — how do I get my records back?**
Simply download Drive Legal, log in with your email and password, and your complete shift history will be restored from the cloud automatically.
